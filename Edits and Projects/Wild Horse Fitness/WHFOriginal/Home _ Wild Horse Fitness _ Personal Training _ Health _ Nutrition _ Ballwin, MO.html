Elite Training Facility in St. Louis

At Wild Horse Fitness our mission is to provide you with all of your health and wellness needs in one place, and lead everyone who comes through our doors to a new level of fitness. We will guide our clients and members with our passion, determination, and knowledge. We strive to assist each individual to meet every goal, one workout at a time. Whether you want to relax after a workout with a massage, try a group fitness class, meet a personal trainer for fitness and nutrition, or have out physical therapist make sure your body is at its best to begin a fitness regiment, you’ll find it at Wild Horse Fitness! Total body wellness begins with you, and it begins today! Contact us or stop by and tour our state-of-the-art facility to see why our members call us the best gym in St. Louis!

Serious Fitness

Located in West County

At Wild Horse Fitness, we help our clients build and achieve the bodies they have always dreamed of having. We focus on developing each individual’s physical state while enhancing his or her mental state, enabling them to live happy, healthy, fulfilled lives. Wild Horse Fitness is located on Kehrs Mill Rd. just south of the Kehrs Mill Rd. and Clayton Rd. intersection.

WILD HORSE FITNESS HAS IT ALL

Wild Horse Fitness is excited to share our completely remodeled 20,000 square-foot facility full of state-of-the-art equipment, a variety of group fitness classes, nationally certified and fully accredited personal trainers, a full service salon, and a smoothie bar that offers healthy meals cooked by FIT4ME FOODS! Wild Horse Fitness offers the latest variety of free weights, plate-loaded equipment, selectorized equipment, and cardio machines. We have a large selection of group fitness classes every week to fit anyone’s desire, not to mention the most experienced personal trainers who can consult you every step of the way on your fitness journey.  The Spa at Wild Horse also offers a full selection of salon services for total relaxation.
